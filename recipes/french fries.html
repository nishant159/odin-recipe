French Fries

Description

French Fries with spice! Derived from firefighters!

Play with the amount of seasonings to best suit your taste and tolerance for heat!

Ingredients

  • â…“ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 2 large russet potatoes - peeled, and sliced into 1/4 inch strips
  • 6 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water. Soak potatoes in water mixture for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove from water, and dry thoroughly on paper towels.
  3. Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
